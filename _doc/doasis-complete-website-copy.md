## Section 1: About the Work

### Q: What's the difference between coaching and counseling?

**A:** Great question‚Äîand one I get often.

**Counseling** is typically focused on healing past wounds, processing trauma, and working through clinical concerns like anxiety, depression, or grief. It's often covered by insurance and follows specific therapeutic modalities.

**Coaching** is future-focused and action-oriented. It's about clarifying goals, navigating transitions, building skills, and creating alignment between your values and your life. Coaching isn't typically covered by insurance and doesn't diagnose or treat mental health conditions.

**At D'Oasis, I offer both**‚Äîand we'll determine together which approach (or blend) serves you best. Sometimes we're doing deep emotional healing (counseling), and sometimes we're strategizing your next chapter (coaching). You get the benefit of both skill sets.
